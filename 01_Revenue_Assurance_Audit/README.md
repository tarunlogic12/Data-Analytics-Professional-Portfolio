# 🔍 SQL Revenue Assurance Audit

Solving the "Missing Data" problem. 

### 🚀 The Challenge
In real-world business environments, data doesn't always align perfectly. I encountered a scenario where customer payment logs were inconsistent with order records. This often leads to "revenue leakage"—where the company loses money without even realizing it.

### 💡 My Approach
I treated this as a forensic audit project. Using **SQL Joins**, I mapped the relationship between fragmented datasets to trace where the data drops were happening. 
* **Logic:** Applied `LEFT JOIN` and `INNER JOIN` strategies to reconcile the discrepancies.
* **Outcome:** Successfully identified the root cause of the payment-order mismatch, ensuring 100% data integrity for the reporting team.

### 🛠️ Technical Toolkit
* **Query Language:** Advanced SQL (Joins, CTEs, Aggregations).
* **Problem Solving:** Data Cleaning & Anomaly Detection.
* **Business Impact:** Revenue protection through precise data mapping.
